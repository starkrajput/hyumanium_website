• Cognitive Psychology

    Explores mental processes including perception, memory, thinking, and problem-solving. This branch examines how we acquire, process, and store information throughout our daily lives.

  • Developmental Psychology

    Studies human growth and changes across the lifespan, from infancy to old age. Focuses on physical, cognitive, emotional, and social development, including how childhood experiences shape adult behavior.

  • Social Psychology

    Examines how social interactions, relationships, and group dynamics influence behavior and cognition. Explores how we perceive ourselves in relation to others and how this affects our choices and beliefs.
