Maritime Compliance Training

Human Element of SIRE 2.0 & RISQ 3.1

Our specialized training focuses on the human element at the core of SIRE 2.0 and RISQ 3.1, equipping seafarers and shore staff to demonstrate competence, communication, and safety culture during inspections.

Participants learn how performance‑influencing factors such as fatigue, stress, and teamwork impact compliance outcomes, and how to proactively manage them.

Through interactive modules, case studies, and scenario‑based exercises, this program strengthens inspection readiness, reduces risks, and builds confidence.

Menopause Support Services
